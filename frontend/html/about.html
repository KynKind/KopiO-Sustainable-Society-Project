Our Story

Kopi-O Sustainable Society was born from a simple observation: students want to make a difference in sustainability, but often don't know where to start or find it overwhelming.

As part of our LSSW6112 Sustainable Society course at Multimedia University, we decided to transform sustainability education into an engaging, gamified experience. The name "Kopi-O" reflects our Malaysian roots while symbolizing the simple, everyday changes that can lead to big impacts.

Our Mission

To make sustainability education accessible, engaging, and rewarding for every MMU student through gamification and community building.

Our Vision

A campus community where every student is empowered to make sustainable choices and become environmental champions in their daily lives.
