About Kopi-O Sustainable Society

Brewing a Sustainable Future, One Game at a Time

Our Story

Kopi-O, where learning about the environment can be fun and engaging! Our goal is to show you what our planet has to offer while keeping things interesting with colourful, meaningful content.

Inspired by the rich tradition of Malaysian coffee that brings people together, we're here to reconnect with nature, just like how a simple coffee bean can shape our daily routines. We want to help you understand the importance of environmental sustainability while honouring the culture that makes Malaysia truly unique.

We believe learning should be rewarding. As you explore more about our environment, you'll collect points that we'll convert into money… and the best part? That money goes directly to WWF Malaysia.

Together, with every point you earn, we can create a greener and healthier community for the future. Working to sustain the natural world for the benefit of people and wildlife.

Our Mission

To make sustainability education accessible, engaging, and rewarding for every MMU student through gamification and community building.

Our Vision

A campus community where every student is empowered to make sustainable choices and become environmental champions in their daily lives.

What We Do

Gamified Learning

Transform sustainability education into fun, interactive games that students actually want to play.

Rewards System

Earn points and redeem real rewards from campus partners for sustainable actions.

Community Building

Connect with like-minded students and compete on our leaderboards.

Impact Tracking

See the real environmental impact of your sustainable choices.
