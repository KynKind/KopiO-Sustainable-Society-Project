Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of sponsorship?

Standard sponsorship agreements are for one academic semester (4-5 months), with options for renewal. We offer discounts for annual commitments.

Can we provide products instead of cash?

Yes! We welcome in-kind sponsorships. Product donations can be used as rewards for students. Contact us to discuss valuation and benefits.

How do you measure impact?

We provide quarterly impact reports including engagement metrics, student feedback, and environmental impact measurements from our games.

Is this tax-deductible?

Yes, as an educational initiative under MMU, sponsorships may qualify as tax-deductible business expenses. We provide official receipts.
