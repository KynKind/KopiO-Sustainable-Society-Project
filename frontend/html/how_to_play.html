Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay to play?

No! Kopi-O Sustainable Society is completely free for all MMU students. We're funded through educational grants and sponsor partnerships.

How often are new games added?

We add new games and challenges every month. Follow our social media for announcements about new content!

Can I play on mobile?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

How do I redeem my points?

Visit your profile page, browse available rewards, and click "Redeem." You'll receive instructions via email.
